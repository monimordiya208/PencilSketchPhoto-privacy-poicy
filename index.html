03

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information depending on your device settings and how you use the app:

a) Photos & Media

  • Images you select from your gallery or capture via camera for sketch conversion.
  • All image processing is performed locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to any server.

b) Device & Usage Information

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (for crash analytics only).
  • App performance data such as crash logs and error reports.
  • General usage statistics (features accessed, session duration) — collected in aggregated, non-identifiable form.

c) Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting support.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, messages, or any data unrelated to the app's core function.
Camera & Storage Permissions: The app requests camera and storage permissions solely to enable photo selection and saving of edited images. These permissions are used only when you actively perform those actions.
04

How We Use Your Data

Any data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the core photo-to-sketch conversion features of the app.
  • To diagnose bugs, improve performance, and fix crashes using anonymized error reports.
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve the user experience in future updates.
  • To respond to your support inquiries if you contact us directly.

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, data brokering, or any purpose not listed above.

Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Photos and images processed by the app are never stored on our servers — they remain on your device.
  • Crash and analytics data is retained for up to 12 months, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Support correspondence is retained for up to 24 months to track ongoing issues.

You may delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device, which removes all locally stored data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to request what personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: The right to restrict the processing of your data.
  • Portability: The right to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: The right to object to data processing under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

Pencil Sketch Photo is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so that we can take necessary action.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete that information promptly from our servers.

Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data we process. These include:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for any network communication.
  • Local-only image processing — your photos never leave your device.
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates.
No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
